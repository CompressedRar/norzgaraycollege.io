Courses

College courses that are available in Norzagaray College. These courses will help you build your future and grow your hidden potential

Bachelor of Computer Science

Awaken your hidden programming skills and let the whole world know you are a programnmer. These course will help you polish your logical and critical thinking skills in building the applications and technologies.

Bachelor of Hospitality Management

A ladderized program that prepares students to have employable skills/competencies at every academic year level for them to be competitive in the hospitality industry locally and globally.

Bachelor of Science in Secondary Education

An undergraduate teacher education program designed to equip learners with adequate and relevant learning competencies to teach Science in the secondary level.

Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education

An undergraduate teacher education degree program structured to prepare individuals intending to teach in the elementary level. It aims to develop highly motivated and competent teachers specializing in the content and pedagogy for elementary education.

Student Organizations

In order to enable students to band together to pursue a shared interest or objective, student organizations have been established at numerous colleges and universities. These groups' various initiatives and activities foster the intellectual and social development of their members.

Norzagaray College Student Council

It is the highest governing student organization of the College, duly recognized and elected by students.

ICONS

Institute of Computer Network Society is an organization that aims to connect and guide students in their CS journey.

HMSoc

Hospitality Management Society aims to instill the values and the mindset needed in a HM student.

SAFE

Skilled and Active Future Educators is an organization that polishes the teaching skills of the students and strengthen their teaching theory that enable them to teach proficiently.
